ABOUT US

A-MOLE is about creating businesses & making functional arrangements to sustain it. We develop businesses that will stay relevant by focusing more on MIS systems, Sales and Marketing. We hire and train knowledge workers to stay in the game. Our focus is on research to build stable systems from diverse sources, especially collective knowledge inside our companies, hence creating a brand culture. We feel very optimistic because we are finally on a sustainable growth path … and it feels good. Mr. Edward Kojo Amole, HEAD of A-MOLE.
